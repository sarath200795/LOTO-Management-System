Privacy Policy

Enterprise Data Protection Protocol

1. Data Encryption & Storage

LOTO PRO Enterprise utilizes Firebase Cloud Encryption to secure all safety procedures. Isolation points, energy source data, and employee identification are encrypted during transit and at rest. Your Organization ID acts as a primary partition key, ensuring your facility data is never visible to other organizations.

2. Personal Identification

We collect limited personal data including Full Name, Email Address, and Occupational Role. This data is used exclusively for the System Audit Trail, allowing the facility owner to track who created, edited, or approved a LOTO procedure. We do not sell or share this data with third-party advertisers.

3. Image & Media Policy

Photos uploaded to isolation points are stored in Base64/Cloud Storage formats. These images are used solely for visual verification of lockout points within your specific Organization ID. Users are responsible for ensuring photos do not capture sensitive proprietary machinery outside the scope of LOTO safety.

4. Access Control

Access is managed through a Two-Tier Authorization system. No user can access Organization data until a verified Owner or Manager has approved their specific Cloud Access Request. Session data is stored in temporary browser storage and is wiped upon manual Logout.

5. Compliance & Safety

This application is a tool for OSHA 1910.147 compliance support. While we protect the integrity of the data, the final verification of isolation always rests with the Authorized Employee performing the lockout.
